## Our Heart for the Community

We believe that faith without works is dead. Our Community Outreach Ministry exists to demonstrate God's love through practical service to our neighbors, especially those facing difficult circumstances.

## Current Programs

### Food & grocery support
**What**: We help connect families facing hunger with groceries or gift cards through partners, donors, and believers in their area — we do not operate a walk-in pantry.

**How to Help**: 
- Give to our mercy fund
- Volunteer for follow-up calls or coordination
- Sponsor a family’s groceries through our contact team

### Community care (from a distance)
**What**: We coordinate practical help—meal support, encouragement, and connecting people with local resources—without operating a physical pantry or dining hall on site.

**How to Help**:
- Give toward mercy and outreach funds
- Volunteer for phone prayer or follow-up
- Share needs through our contact page so we can respond

### Practical needs
**What**: When possible, we help connect families with clothing, food, or other resources through partner ministries and believers in their own communities.

**How to Help**:
- Donate toward designated outreach offerings
- Offer to meet a need in your own neighborhood as an extension of this ministry

### Senior Care Ministry
**What**: Encouragement and prayer for elderly viewers and their families, plus help connecting them with trusted helpers in their own town when practical needs arise.

**Services** (as volunteers are available):
- Phone calls and prayer
- Coordinating meal or errand help through local contacts
- Sharing online worship and resource links

## Seasonal Outreach

### Back-to-School Drive
- Collect school supplies for local students
- Provide backpacks and clothing
- Partner with local schools

### Thanksgiving Baskets
- Provide complete Thanksgiving meals
- Deliver to families in need
- Include personal notes of encouragement

### Christmas Toy Drive
- Collect new toys for children
- Partner with local organizations
- Organize gift wrapping events

### Winter Coat Drive
- Collect warm clothing for all ages
- Distribute through community partners
- Focus on children and elderly

## Community Partnerships

We work closely with:
- Local food banks
- Homeless shelters
- Senior centers
- Public schools
- Other churches and faith organizations

## Volunteer Opportunities

### Ways to serve remotely:
- Prayer, phone follow-up, and encouragement
- Coordinating practical help through partners and local believers
- Administrative support (email, scheduling, social posts)
- Fundraising and awareness for mercy projects

### Special opportunities:
- Seasonal giving campaigns
- Online events and teaching
- Sharing needs and testimonies (with permission)

## How to Get Involved

1. **Contact us**: Tell us your gifts and availability through our contact page.
2. **Choose your lane**: We’ll suggest prayer, giving, coordination, or local partnership options.
3. **Brief orientation**: For roles that need it, we’ll walk you through expectations online.
4. **Start serving**: Bless others from where you are — no building shift required.

## Donation Needs

Gifts are used for mercy, outreach, and connecting people with tangible help. Specific lists are shared when drives are announced on our **events** page and social channels — we do not maintain a public drop-off address on this site.
